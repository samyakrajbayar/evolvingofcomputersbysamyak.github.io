COMPUTERS IN DIFFERENT AREAS


Let us learn more about the uses of computers in different areas.


At Home


Computers are used at home for the following:


  • Playing games
  • Listening to music
  • Performing calculations
  • Storing addresses and telephone numbers
  • Writing letters
  • Keeping track of monthly expenses
  • Watching movie

At Schools


Computers are used in schools by teachers:


  • Preparing lessons
  • Teaching different subjects using CDs

    • Students also use computers for:

  • Preparing projects
  • Learning different subjects

    • In making Films


    • Earlier, creating animation films used to be a difficult task. Each picture in the sequence had to be drawn by hand. Today, computers have made it really easy to make cartoon films and other animations
    • Animation is the technique of giving an object the appearance of movement by rapidly running a series of images of the object or character in different positions
    • Pictures can be easily modified nowadays on a computer to suit the requirements of a film.
    • Special effects such as the sequence of a battle, an earthquake, or a fire can be created using a computer
    • These special effects can also be combined with real scenes and characters when needed

    In Space Technology


    Computers are widely used in space technology


    • Computers are used to monitor and control space equipment satellites, etc.
    • Satellites are linked to computers, which provide information for space research
    • Satellites also help predict weather patterns. Satellites transfer information from space to computers on Earth These computers process the information, and make predictions about the weather.
    • Space shuttles and rockets, which take man into space, are controlled by computers.

    At Offices


    Computers are used in offices for the following:


    • Maintaining office records
    • Communicating through e-mails
    • Typing and printing documents

    in Libraries


    Computers are used in libraries for the following:


    • Maintaining the database of all the books available in the library
    • Keeping track of issue and return of books.

    In Medicine


    Computers are widely used in the field of medicine


    • Computers are used to maintain medical records patients for future reference.
    • Listening to music
    • Performing calculations
    • Computerized equipment is used for performing medical tests and surgeries in hospitals.
    • Computer-assisted equipment helps in monitoring the condition of patients
    • Computerized machines are used to manufacture

    In Banks


    • Providing account details to customers and in printing account statements.
    • Operating Automated Teller Machines (ATM which are machines installed outside the bank that enable customers to withdraw money)

    In Designing


    Computers are used in designing:


    • Architects design buildings and houses on computers
    • Engineers design cars, aeroplanes, etc., on computers
    • A dress designer designs dresses on a computer
    • Computers are used to create documents such as newsletters and books for commercial printing. This is called desktop publishing

    In Shops and Hotels


    Computers are used in shops and hotels for the following:


    • Maintaining records of stocks and prices of items.
    • Preparing bills

    in Airline and Railway Reservation offices


    Computers are used in airline and railway booking counters where all computers are connected to a common network This enables people to book tickets from any towns city to any other place. The steps for booking a ticket are as follows:


    • All the information required is fed into a computer by the booking personnel
    • The availability of tickets is checked. The tickets so booked are printed and issued
    • This new information is updated immediately on all computers through the common network and the latest status is displayed.
